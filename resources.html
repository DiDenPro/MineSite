About resource packs

Resource packs are game modifications that allow us to change the original textures of the game

If you ever dreamed to change the look of the game, you should make it. Change the look of the game by installing the resource packs.

ItemBound

This texturpack adds new items, and you heard it right, items with their own names! In order to create an item from the pack, you will need to look at the recipe, take the original item and rename it in the anvil, to the name specified in the recipe. At the moment, 1,681 items are available for Minecraft 1.13.2.
